# Ghost Job Finder

AI powered job matcher that ranks job listings against your profile and recommends top opportunities.

## What It Does
- Loads job listings with requirements and salary
- Matches against candidate profile
- Scores each job out of 100
- Identifies gaps to address
- Recommends top 3 jobs to apply first
- Sends ranked digest via email

## Sample Results
Top match: AWS Solutions Architect at Accenture Irving TX
